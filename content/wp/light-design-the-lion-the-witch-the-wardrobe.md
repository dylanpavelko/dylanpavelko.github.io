(The show runs through the end of January and tickets can be bought at the SLOLT’s website <http://slolittletheatre.org/pages>, and then click Tickets)

I did the light design for the show (the first show I’ve LDed by myself in… a year? It’s been soo long!) During one of the last rehearsals I snapped some shots to archive/post/promote the show with, so I thought I’d go ahead and post them here. (It is a rehearsal so ignore the unfinished parts of the set.)

Enjoy!
